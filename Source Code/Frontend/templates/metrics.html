Model Insights

  • Highest accuracy: 91.46% achieved by the stacked ensemble model.
  • Recall (Fire Class): 69%, ensuring most fire days are correctly predicted.
  • ROC AUC: 0.87, indicating strong discriminative power.
  • NDVI Trends: Fire activity peaks when NDVI dips, confirming vegetation dryness correlation.
  • Balanced sensitivity: SMOTE technique reduced bias towards non-fire samples.
